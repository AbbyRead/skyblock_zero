
# Questline: Emittrium Reactor

Emittrium reactors be another way of generatin' energy. They be a 3x3x3 cube made of reactor shells (or reactor glass), with a reactor core in the center. Some of the shells must be replaced with a reactor power port, a reactor coolant port, a reactor emittrium port, and a reactor infoscreen. It consumes emittrium and water.
If it doesn't have enough coolant (water), it heats up, and bad things happen if it heats up too much.

## Reactor Shells
### ID: qid_reactor_shells

### Text

Reactor Shells be needed fer craftin' the emittrium reactor parts and fer construction of the emittrium reactor.  
Ye can also use 'em as a nice decorative block that won't explode to meteorites.

### Meta

Requires: Obtain Emittrium

## Reactor Glass
### ID: qid_reactor_glass

### Text

Reactor Glass be needed in craftin' recipes and can optionally be used as a replacement fer the reactor shell in the construction of the emittrium reactor.  
Ye can also use it as a nice decorative block that won't explode to meteorites.

### Meta

Requires: qid_emittrium_glass, qid_reactor_shells

## Reactor Infoscreens
### ID: qid_reactor_infoscreens

### Text

A Reactor Infoscreen be needed in the construction of the emittrium reactor and be required to turn on the reactor.

### Meta

Requires: qid_reactor_glass

## Reactor Power Ports
### ID: qid_reactor_power_ports

### Text

A Reactor Power Port be needed in the construction of the emittrium reactor. It delivers the energy generated by the reactor core.

### Meta

Requires: qid_reactor_shells

## Reactor Coolant Ports
### ID: qid_reactor_coolant_ports

### Text

A Reactor Coolant Port be needed in the construction of the emittrium reactor. It needs 1 water bucket per second (delivered via pipes) to sustain reactor heat.

### Meta

Requires: qid_fluid_storage_tanks

## Reactor Emittrium Input
### ID: qid_reactor_emittrium_input

### Text

A Reactor Emittrium Input be needed in the construction of the emittrium reactor. It needs 16 emittrium per minute to fuel the reactor.

### Meta

Requires: qid_reactor_shells, qid_tubes

## Reactor Core
### ID: qid_reactor_core

### Text

A Reactor Core be needed in the construction of the emittrium reactor. It needs to be in the center.

### Meta

Requires: qid_neutronium, qid_reactor_shells

## Building the emittrium reactor and turning it on
### ID: qid_building_the_emittrium_reactor_and_turning_it_on

### Text

Build a 3x3x3 cube of reactor shells or reactor glass. In the center, place the core, but in place of some of the shells, build the extra blocks mentioned earlier (Emittrium Input, Coolant Port, Power Port, etc.).  
  
Now, to actually turn the reactor on, click the Infoscreen and turn it on in the UI. If somethin' be wrong with the reactor, it'll tell ye.  
  
If ye have multiple reactors, try pressin' the re-link button if ye think the Infoscreen be displayin' the wrong information.  
The finished reactor should look somethin' like this (obviously, there be many more ways to build it than just this):

In older releases, the emittrium reactor used to explode when it ran out of coolant, but this be no longer the case.

### Meta
